About CIPT

CIPT (Comprehensive Investment Planning Tool) is a free application designed to help individuals manage their finances effectively. With CIPT, users can track their investments, plan their financial goals, and gain insights into their financial health.

Whether you are a seasoned investor or just starting out, CIPT provides the tools you need to make informed decisions and achieve your financial objectives.
